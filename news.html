🎤 Legendary Duo Clipse Coming to Cleveland — The Return of Real Rap:

Date & Venue:
🗓️ Date to be announced
📍 Location: TBA — stay locked in for updates

Cleveland hip hop heads, get ready for a night of history — because the legendary duo Clipse is touching down in the 216.
Known for their razor-sharp lyricism, hard-hitting production, and street-centered storytelling, Clipse — composed of brothers Pusha T and No Malice— carved their names into hip hop history with early 2000s classics like Grindin, When the Last Time, and the cult-favorite album Hell Hath No Fury. After years apart and successful solo runs, the two are finally reuniting for select performances — and Cleveland just made the list.
This show isn’t just a concert — it’s a moment. With production from the Neptunes and bars that shaped a generation, Clipse helped redefine the sound of street rap. Now, with hip hop in a new era, their return brings something that fans — young and old — have been craving: raw, unfiltered authenticity. Whether you grew up with Lord Willin on repeat or you just discovered Pusha T through his solo work, this is your chance to witness legends live and direct. Expect gritty energy, polished delivery, and a crowd full of real ones. **Local love**: Rumors are already swirling about surprise guest appearances and local acts opening the show. If you're an artist trying to get on that radar, now’s the time to move strategically — submit your music, build your buzz, and show why Cleveland deserves a spotlight. Stay tapped in right here on Cleveland Hip Hop Magazine for official announcements, ticket info, and exclusive coverage when the night finally goes down.
