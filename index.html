Cleveland Hip Hop Insider

About Us

Cleveland Hip Hop Magazine is a digital platform dedicated to spotlighting the raw energy, real voices, and rising stars of Cleveland’s hip hop culture. We’re more than just a blog — we’re the digital stage for the 216. From underground artists grinding in home studios to sold-out shows downtown, our mission is to document the movement, uplift the scene, and give shine to the talent that’s often overlooked by the mainstream. We feature exclusive interviews, mixtape drops, event coverage, artist profiles, and cultural commentary. Whether you're a rapper, DJ, promoter, producer, or just a hip hop head — this is your space. Powered by the streets, curated with love, and built for the culture. If you're from Cleveland or rep it in your bars, we want to hear from you.

Featured Artist

  • Rae Rozay Gully

    Rae Rozay Gully isn’t just another name in the Cleveland rap game — he’s a voice for the trenches, a storyteller for the struggle, and a force pushing real bars back into the spotlight. Raised on the east side of Cleveland, Rae Rozay came up in an environment that shaped both his grind and his sound. His music is unapologetically raw, blending street wisdom with emotional depth, clever punchlines, and a delivery that demands attention. Whether it’s pain, loyalty, hustle, or survival, Rae paints vivid pictures through his lyrics — no cap, no filter. With a growing catalog of mixtapes and visuals under his belt, Rae Rozay has become a familiar face at open mics, local showcases, and underground cyphers. But what sets him apart isn’t just his bars — it’s his authenticity. He speaks what he lives and lives what he speaks. Rae is a front-row witness to the realities of Cleveland life, and through music, he gives listeners a glimpse into that world — not for clout, but for connection. He’s about substance, not stunts. Whether you’re hearing him for the first time or you’ve been following the wave, Rae Rozay Gully is a name that belongs in the conversation when talking about the future of Ohio hip hop.   • Boomy D

    Boomy D is a seasoned lyricist with 15 years in the game — and he’s still sharpening his sword. Hailing from the heart of the Midwest, Boomy brings a sound that’s rooted in raw experience, emotional depth, and lyrical precision. His music lives where vulnerability meets power. Inspired by the intensity of Tech N9ne, the introspection of J. Cole, and the emotional honesty of NF, Boomy D delivers more than just bars — he delivers messages. Every verse he spits comes from real life: the highs, the lows, the grind, the growth. With over a decade in the booth, Boomy’s style has evolved into something unique — fast when it needs to be, deep when it matters, and always 100% real. Whether he’s unpacking pain or flexing lyrical agility, Boomy D doesn’t just rap — he connects. This isn’t music for the moment. This is music for the ones who’ve been through it, who feel every line, and who live life bar by bar.
